Your First Visit

Your first appointment will involve a consultation with one of our dentists. During this process, we will collect as much information as possible about your child’s general medical health and oral health, which may include any family history of dental disease, past treatment experiences and specific concerns you may have.

To make your first visit a smoother process, please download and complete the form below.

If your child has previously had X-rays taken, please bring them with you.

Preparing your child for their first Dental Visit

Your child’s first dental visit with the pediatric dentist should be an enjoyable, pleasant, and safe visit.

To prepare for this initial visit let your child know the dentist will ask the parent some questions and count the teeth. Try to stay away from words that conjure pain and distress, such as pain, needle, injection, pinch, ‘hurt just a little’ etc.

The examination period of the initial appointment is usually short and quite easy to perform. Please let you child know that the initial visit will be a short and very pleasant experience. Once the initial examination part is over, the pediatric dentist will then speak with the parents and formulate a treatment plan.
