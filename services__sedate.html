Our Services

Treatment under sedation or general anaesthesia

Some procedures require the use of sedation or general anaesthesia on your child to give them a pain free experience. In our procedures we use the following safe methods of sedation:

Nitrous Oxide

Nitrous oxide or happy gas can help some children feel more relaxed and comfortable. While receiving happy gas your child will remain completely awake and responsive

General Anaesthesia

General anaesthesia provides dental treatment while the child is asleep. Children who require extensive dental treatment or are not able to tolerate treatment comfortable in a normal practice setting. General anaesthesia is always administered by a specialist anaesthetist, who is highly skilled and trained in this area.
