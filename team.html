Our Team

Dr Peter Wong

BDS (Hons), MDSc, Dip Clin Dent(sedation), FRACDS, FICD, FADI, FPFA, Juris Doctor

Sepcialist Paediatric Dentist

Peter graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Dentistry (Honours) in 1985 and subsequently a Master’s Degree in Paediatric Dentistry in 1991 from the University of Sydney. Peter founded Canberra Paediatric Dentistry in Deakin, ACT in 1992. He has recently completed a Juris Doctor degree from the University of Canberra and was admitted as a lawyer in the Supreme Court of the ACT in 2019.Peter’s interests in dentistry are varied with particular emphasis on management of trauma, dental anomalies and dental sedation

Dr Fiona Bell

BDS, MDSc, FICD, FADI

Sepcialist Paediatric Dentist

Fiona obtained her Bachelor of Dentistry from the University of Melbourne in 1994 as well as her Master’s Degree in Paediatric Dentistry from the University of Sydney in 2006.

She has been at the Canberra Paediatric Dentistry since 2007.

Dr Wendy Lou

BDS(USyd), MScDent(USyd)

General Dentist

Dr Wendy Lou grew up in Canberra and is an alumni of The University of Sydney, where she obtained her undergraduate dental degree in 1988, and postgraduate degree in molecular genetics in 2005. She has worked mostly in private practices. She is a The University of Sydney dental school clinical tutor, a position she has held for the past decade. She has a particular interest in cosmetics and orthodontics, where she finds the ability to change someone’s smile and improving their confidence as most rewarding.

Her hobbies and interests range from the arts, music, travel, yoga and DIY projects.
