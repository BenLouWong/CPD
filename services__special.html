Our Services

Special Needs

Paediatric dentists are trained in the management of children with special needs. Whether they are anxious, have a medical, or diagnosed genetic condition, paediatric dentists are well experienced in the management of dental disease in these children.

Children with special needs often require treatment in hospitals, and in certain cases in conjunction with other specialists such as paediatric surgeons or ENT surgeons joint procedures can be performed concurrently.
